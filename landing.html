Turn Any Map into Usable Data

Map Extract lets you upload a scanned map or PDF, georeference it, extract features like water bodies, and export to GIS formats like GeoJSON and Shapefile.

How It Works

1. Upload Your Map

Upload any scanned or digital map in PDF format.

2. Extract Features

Isolate water, landforms, contours, and more — automatically.

3. Export Data

Download GIS-ready data (GeoJSON or SHP) for your projects.

Features

Water Body Extraction

Automatically convert lakes and rivers from scanned maps into real GIS polygons.

Georeferencing Tools

Add ground control points and compute affine transforms without desktop GIS software.

Export to GIS

Export your extracted shapes to GeoJSON or Shapefile for use in QGIS, ArcGIS, and more.

Legend-Aware AI (Coming Soon)

Automatically detect feature types directly from the map legend using AI.
