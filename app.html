How to Use

  1. Select a PDF map file from your computer.
  2. Wait for the map to render in the preview area.
  3. Click Extract Water to detect water regions.
  4. Review the highlighted water overlay on the map.
  5. Click Download GeoJSON to export the water features.
  6. Open the GeoJSON in QGIS, ArcGIS, or your GIS tool of choice.

Tip: For best results, start with maps where water is clearly drawn in blue and the map image is reasonably high resolution.
